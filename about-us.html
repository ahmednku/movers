Our History

Founded in 2010, Movers started with a simple vision: to provide top-quality moving services in Jeddah. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in the industry, known for our commitment to customer satisfaction and our ability to handle moves of all sizes and complexities.

Timeline

  • 2010: Movers was established with a focus on local moving services.
  • 2012: Expanded services to include long-distance moves and corporate relocations.
  • 2015: Introduced specialized packing and storage solutions to cater to diverse customer needs.
  • 2018: Upgraded fleet and technology for enhanced efficiency and customer experience.
  • 2022: Celebrated a decade of excellence with numerous awards and recognitions in the industry.
